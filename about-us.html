About Us

When a strong team comes together and wants to drive customer value.

Shanghai Blue Orange Science and Trade Co., Ltd, is an Managed IT solution and Services provider. Blue Orange was officialy founded in Shanghai in 2013.  Headquartered in Shanghai, Blue Orange has established several strategic overseas partnerships in Europe to offer world-class solutions and 24H service.

Our core philopshy is enabling local and international start-ups and SME/SMB companies to have first hand access to local and international IT resources. Through our MSP concept, we are commited to become a long-term number one player in Shanghai and the Benelux for IT solutions and Services for companies that are looking for a one contact IT service and solution partner.

Within the modern IT cloud and mobile first environment, we test and implement solutions and services that enable business to maximize the ROI of their IT investments. Due to our knowledge and experience, Blue Orange is able to help businesses decide, based on fundemental facts, which IT solutions and services work best in China and overseas.

Blue Orange HQ is located in the Songjiang Caohejing High Tech Park. This park includes businesses like Google, Aliyun, Mi, and other top 500 companies in China. Due to our location, we have direct access to our technology partners and providers.
